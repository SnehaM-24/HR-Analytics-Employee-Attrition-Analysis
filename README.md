# 👨‍💼 HR Analytics – Employee Attrition Prediction & Workforce Insights

## 📌 Project Overview

The **HR Analytics – Employee Attrition Prediction & Workforce Insights** project is an end-to-end data analytics and machine learning solution developed to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, employee satisfaction, compensation, and organizational performance.

The project follows a complete analytics workflow, beginning with data cleaning and exploratory data analysis (EDA) in Python, followed by feature engineering and machine learning model development, SQL-based business analysis, and finally an interactive Power BI dashboard for business decision-making.

This project demonstrates practical skills required for **Data Analyst, HR Analyst, Business Analyst, and Junior Data Scientist** roles in MNCs, consulting firms, and startups.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Employee attrition is one of the biggest challenges faced by organizations. High employee turnover increases recruitment costs, affects productivity, reduces employee morale, and impacts overall business performance.

Without proper data analysis, HR teams struggle to identify:

* Employees at high risk of leaving
* Departments with high attrition
* Impact of overtime on attrition
* Salary and compensation trends
* Employee satisfaction levels
* Career growth patterns
* Workforce demographics
* Key factors driving employee turnover

The objective of this project is to transform HR data into actionable business insights and build a predictive model that helps organizations improve employee retention.

# 📌 Business Insights

* Overtime employees experienced a higher attrition rate than employees without overtime.
* Departments and job roles showed different attrition patterns, helping identify high-risk workforce segments.
* Employees with lower job satisfaction and work-life balance were more likely to leave the organization.
* Monthly income and career growth significantly influenced employee retention.
* Employees with fewer years at the company contributed a large share of total attrition.
* Workforce demographics provided valuable insights into employee distribution across departments and job roles.
* Feature importance analysis identified the key variables influencing employee attrition.
* Machine learning models enabled prediction of employee attrition, supporting proactive HR decision-making.
